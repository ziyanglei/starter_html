Swedish Pancakes

Source: Family Recipe Category: Dessert

Recipe Ingredients

Pancake Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Unsalted butter (melted)

Recipe Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the egg and then add the milk and melted butter. Mix until well combined.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are okay.
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium-high heat and lightly grease it with butter or cooking spray.
  5. For each pancake, pour a ladleful of the batter onto the skillet.
  6. Cook until you see bubbles forming on the surface, then flip the pancakes with a spatula and cook until golden brown on both sides.
  7. Repeat the process for the remaining batter, adding more butter or oil to the skillet as needed.
  8. Serve your delicious pancakes with your favorite toppings, such as maple syrup, fresh berries, or whipped cream.

Italian Spaghetti

Source: Family Recipe Category: Main Dish

Recipe Ingredients

  • Unsalted butter
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Spaghetti noodle
  • Ground Pork
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Sea Salt
  • Fresh Basil or Italian Seasoning
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Freshly Ground Black Pepper

Recipe Steps

  1. Boil a large pot of water and add a pinch of salt.
  2. Place spaghetti noodles in the boiling water and cook until al dente (8-12 minutes).
  3. Drain the cooked pasta in a colander.
  4. In a separate skillet, heat olive oil and sauté finely chopped onions and garlic until translucent.
  5. If using meat, add ground beef or Italian sausage to the skillet and cook until browned.
  6. Pour in tomato sauce, season with salt, black pepper, and optionally red pepper flakes. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  7. Combine the cooked pasta with the sauce, garnish with fresh basil or Italian seasoning, and serve hot. Optionally, add grated Parmesan cheese and a drizzle of olive oil.

Guacamole

Source: Family Recipe Category: Diping Sauce

Recipe Ingredients

  • Ripe Avocados
  • Red Onion
  • Fresh Cilantro
  • Jalapeño Pepper
  • Lime Juice
  • Garlic Clove
  • Tomato
  • Ground Cumin (optional)
  • Salt
  • Freshly Ground Black Pepper

Recipe Steps

  1. Choose ripe avocados and cut them in half. Remove the seeds and scoop the flesh into a mixing bowl.
  2. Finely chop red onion, fresh cilantro, jalapeño pepper, and garlic clove. Add them to the bowl with the avocados.
  3. Squeeze fresh lime juice over the mixture to taste. This adds flavor and helps prevent the avocados from browning.
  4. If desired, add a pinch of ground cumin for extra flavor.
  5. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.
  6. Gently mash and mix all the ingredients together until you reach your desired guacamole consistency.
  7. Chop a tomato and fold it into the guacamole mixture. This adds a fresh and juicy element.
  8. Optional: For some heat, you can sprinkle in some red pepper flakes.
  9. Serve your freshly made guacamole with tortilla chips, as a dip, or as a topping for your favorite Mexican dishes.
